Quick Start
Three commands. Zero config.
Install, deliver, consolidate. That's the whole workflow.
01 — install
$ claude install hypership
Reads your CLAUDE.md. Adapts to your stack. Done.
02 — deliver
$ /delivery add roles to auth
Classifies, plans, tests, ships. Your plugins, your pipeline.
03 — consolidate
$ /removedebt last 3 features
Scans real debt. You decide. Safety gates protect you.
Philosophy
A delivery-first methodology.
Features and debt are separate.
Don't refactor during feature delivery. Ship first, consolidate deliberately.
Debt is concrete, not speculative.
Grep-able duplication, actual unused code. Not "could be more generic".
Senior engineers decide.
Tool proposes, you dispose. Every finding has a skip option.
